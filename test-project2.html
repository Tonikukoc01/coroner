ARTMO Masterclass Implementation

Delivered live, self-hosted masterclasses to 5,000+ global viewers, making artist education accessible and boosting platform revenue by 25%.

Overview

At ARTMO, a social e-commerce platform for artists and art lovers, I took the lead on both the technical and operational sides of launching a new live masterclass feature. The goal was to provide high-quality, accessible education while creating a new revenue stream. We needed a reliable, scalable streaming setup that integrated smoothly with the platform and could support thousands of viewers without lag. This is the story of how we built it and what it achieved.

The Challenge

We identified a significant opportunity to expand our platform's reach by offering high quality educational content from our established artists & art educators. However, implementing this feature presented several technical challenges:

Scalable Streaming Infrastructure

We needed to build a streaming solution capable of handling hundreds of concurrent viewers without compromising video quality or experiencing buffering issues, while keeping infrastructure costs low and ensuring the content could be hosted on the platform for long-term access.

Interactive Learning Experience

Simply streaming video wasn't enough. The platform needed to support real-time live chat, post-class Q&A, and other interactive elements to create an engaging learning environment.

E-commerce Integration

The masterclasses needed to work with the old payment system, enabling different promotions and a variety of income options like subscriptions, free trials, and package deals.

Approach

March 2024
Competition Research & Requirements Gathering
We looked at what competitors were offering and spoke to users to understand the key features and issues in existing art education platforms. Based on what we learned, we put together clear technical requirements for the project.
April-May 2024
Architecture Design & Prototyping
We set up a cloud-based streaming system using AWS IVS for low-latency streaming. Built prototypes for interactive features and planned how to integrate e-commerce functionality. We also worked on transitioning to playable S3 files and reused existing modules for the interactive elements.
Summer 2024
Development & Testing
Built the streaming backend and interactive features, and developed a user-friendly dashboard for content managers to easily create, stream, and integrate content. Worked to ensure the system could support a high number of concurrent viewers.
September 2024
Beta Launch & Artist Onboarding
Launched the first masterclasses with previously instructed artists and 500 users via bulk messaging. Collected feedback on user experience and identified issues with the architecture for improvement.
October-December 2024
Full Launch & Scaling
Officially launched the scalable and functional masterclass platform to all ARTMO users. Integrated analytics and continuously optimized performance while adding new features based on user feedback.

Key Implementations

Low-Latency Streaming Architecture

Implemented AWS Interactive Video Service (IVS) with multi-region distribution to ensure low-latency, high-quality streaming for users worldwide. This was complemented by dynamic quality adaptation based on viewer bandwidth capabilities.

Real-Time Interaction Engine

Developed an interaction system using a combination of WebSocket and IVS Chat, enabling real-time polls, Q&A, and highlighted comments to create an engaging classroom environment.

Integrated E-commerce Experience

Integrated the existing e-commerce experience with masterclass content, allowing viewers to purchase related products directly within the stream. This opened new income opportunities, including free promotions, discount codes, subscription options, and the ability to purchase individual masterclasses.

Dashboard for Instructors

Developed a user-friendly dashboard designed to easily onboard new instructors, enabling them to integrate, manage, and host their own masterclasses on the platform without hassle.

User engagement metrics showing increased interaction rates and time spent on platform after masterclass implementation.

Results & Impact

The masterclass implementation delivered exceptional results across key metrics:

Platform Growth

Attracted over 1,000 viewers across three masterclasses within the first month, with almost no marketing spend. Masterclass content became one of the most engaging features on the platform, with average session lengths of 45+ minutes compared to just 12 minutes for regular content.

Revenue Impact

Increased platform revenue by 25% through a combination of subscription fees and pay-per-view access. Sales of products featured in masterclasses saw a significant increase compared to standard listings, becoming a key source of income.

Technical Excellence

Achieved exceptional stream reliability for minimal cost, with automatic transfer to S3 and CloudFront ensuring smooth delivery and minimal buffering issues.

User Growth

Offered free masterclasses for first-timers, driving a 45% increase in user growth during Q4 of 2024. This initiative became a key factor in boosting our most important metric: user growth.

Conclusion

The ARTMO Masterclass implementation is a major technical achievement that delivered lasting business value at a minimal development cost. By combining efficient streaming technology, intuitive UX design, and e-commerce integration, we created a sustainable, profitable product that continues to make money.
