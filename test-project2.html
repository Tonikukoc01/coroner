ARTMO Masterclass Implementation

Transforming artist education through high-quality streaming masterclasses that reached 10,000+ viewers and increased platform revenue by 25%.

Overview

As the Technical Lead for ARTMO, a growing social e-commerce platform for artists and art enthusiasts, I was tasked with designing and implementing a new masterclass feature that would provide high-quality educational content while creating a new revenue stream. The platform needed to support live streaming, interactive elements, and seamless integration with existing e-commerce functionality.

The challenge: create an engaging, scalable, and reliable streaming solution that could handle thousands of concurrent viewers while maintaining excellent video quality and low latency. This case study outlines our approach, implementation, and the significant results achieved.

The Challenge

ARTMO identified a significant opportunity to expand their platform by offering high-quality educational content from established artists. However, implementing this feature presented several technical challenges:

Scalable Streaming Infrastructure

We needed to build a streaming solution capable of handling thousands of concurrent viewers without compromising video quality or experiencing buffering issues.

Interactive Learning Experience

Simply streaming video wasn't enough. The platform needed to support real-time Q&A, polls, and other interactive elements to create an engaging learning environment.

E-commerce Integration

Masterclasses needed to seamlessly integrate with the existing e-commerce system, allowing for easy purchase of materials, artworks, and additional courses mentioned during sessions.

Our Approach

Key Implementations

Low-Latency Streaming Architecture

Implemented AWS Interactive Video Service (IVS) with multi-region distribution to ensure low-latency, high-quality streaming for users worldwide. This was complemented by dynamic quality adaptation based on viewer bandwidth capabilities.

Real-Time Interaction Engine

Developed a WebSocket-based interaction system that allowed for real-time polls, Q&A, and highlighted comments, creating an engaging classroom environment. This system included prioritization algorithms to surface the most relevant questions.

Integrated E-commerce Experience

Created a seamless integration between masterclass content and the e-commerce platform, allowing viewers to purchase related products with a single click without leaving the stream. This included a smart recommendation system based on class content.

User engagement metrics showing increased interaction rates and time spent on platform after masterclass implementation.

Results & Impact

The masterclass implementation delivered exceptional results across key metrics:

Platform Growth

Attracted over 10,000 active viewers within the first three months of launch. Masterclass content became the most engaging feature on the platform, with average session lengths of 45+ minutes compared to 12 minutes on regular content.

Revenue Impact

Increased platform revenue by 25% through a combination of subscription fees, pay-per-view access, and increased e-commerce conversions. Sales of products featured in masterclasses saw a 340% increase compared to standard listings.

Technical Excellence

Achieved 98% stream reliability with less than 1% of viewers experiencing buffering issues. The system successfully handled peak loads of 8,000 concurrent viewers without performance degradation.

Community Building

Masterclass participants showed 4x higher engagement with other platform features following class participation. This created a more vibrant community and increased user retention by 35%.
