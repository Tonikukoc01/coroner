FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the ARTMO verification badge?

The ARTMO verification badge is a mark of authenticity that appears on your profile. It helps build trust with potential buyers by showing that your identity has been verified.

How long does verification last?

Once you've been verified, your verification badge will remain on your profile indefinitely. It's a one-time payment for lifetime verification.

How does the verification process work?

After payment, our team will review your profile within 24-48 hours. Once approved, the verification badge will automatically appear on your profile.

Is the verification fee refundable?

If your verification is not approved, we'll issue a full refund. Otherwise, the verification fee is non-refundable.
