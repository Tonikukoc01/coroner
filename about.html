About me.

I'm an IT manager with a background in business economics and analytics.
I am passionate about startups and traveling,
and navigating the ever-changing challenges of the tech world.

SCROLL

I grew up in a small village on the Dalmatian coast, surrounded by the remnants of a quiet, agricultural life. It is a place where tradition rans deep, but my curiosity always pulled me toward something different. When I wasn’t playing basketball, I was glued to my computer, fascinated by the world of technology.

University in Split introduced me to Croatia’s startup scene—a world of bold risk-takers and fast-moving builders. That energy stuck with me. After finishing my master’s across Portugal and Spain, I came back to Split to wrap up my studies before heading to Lisbon, one of Europe’s startup hotspots, to see what I could make of it.

These days, I’m still based in Lisbon, working in the ever-changing world of IT always looking for new challenges in a field that never sits still.

fitness & nutrition enthusiast

With over a decade spent in the gym and hundreds of hours researching body mechanics and nutrition, fitness has always been more than just a hobby for me. I’m fascinated by how small adjustments in movement, diet, and recovery can make a huge difference in performance and well-being.

These days, I’m especially interested in how technology can enhance fitness and nutrition. With countless apps and trackers flooding the market, I’m exploring ways tech can cut through the noise—offering real insights that help people build sustainable, science-backed habits instead of chasing trends.

On the move

I’ve visited 20 countries, explored hundreds of European cities, and lived in four different countries along the way. Experiencing new cultures, meeting people from different backgrounds, and constantly adapting to new environments keeps life exciting.
